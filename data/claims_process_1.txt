Insurance Claims Process - Step by Step Guide
This document outlines the standard process for filing and settling insurance claims for all policy types including health, vehicle, home, and life insurance.

Step 1 - Notify the Insurer:
- Inform the insurance company within 24-48 hours of the incident
- Call the 24/7 claims helpline or submit online through the portal
- Note down the claim reference number provided

Step 2 - Document the Incident:
- Take photographs of the damage or incident scene
- Collect police reports for theft, accident, or crime related claims
- Gather medical reports and bills for health claims
- Preserve all original receipts and invoices

Step 3 - Submit the Claim Form:
- Fill out the claim form accurately and completely
- Attach all required supporting documents
- Submit within the stipulated time period (usually 7-30 days)

Step 4 - Claim Assessment:
- A surveyor or assessor will be assigned within 48 hours
- Cooperate fully with the investigation process
- Provide any additional documents requested promptly

Step 5 - Settlement:
- Approved claims are settled within 7-30 working days
- Payment is made directly to the hospital, garage, or policyholder
- Cashless claims are settled directly with the service provider

Key Tips:
- Always read your policy document before filing a claim
- Keep copies of all submitted documents
- Follow up regularly on claim status
