Home Insurance Policy - Property Protection Cover
This home insurance policy provides comprehensive protection for your residential property against damage, theft, and natural disasters. The policy covers up to 50 lakhs for structural damage and 10 lakhs for contents.

Key Benefits:
- Coverage for fire, flood, earthquake, and storm damage
- Theft and burglary protection for household contents
- Temporary accommodation expenses up to Rs. 10,000 per day
- Third party liability coverage up to Rs. 5 lakhs
- Personal accident cover for residents

Exclusions:
- Damage due to wear and tear
- Loss due to war or nuclear activity
- Damage caused by pets
- Electrical or mechanical breakdown

Validity: 1 year from policy start date
Premium: Rs. 8,000 per year
