Business Insurance Policy - Commercial Coverage
This business insurance policy provides comprehensive protection for commercial establishments including property damage, liability, and business interruption coverage.

Key Benefits:
- Commercial property coverage up to Rs. 2 crores
- Business interruption coverage for loss of income up to 12 months
- Public liability coverage up to Rs. 50 lakhs
- Employee theft and fidelity coverage
- Product liability protection
- Cyber liability basic coverage included

Exclusions:
- Intentional damage or fraud by owner
- Damage due to poor maintenance
- Loss due to market fluctuations
- Contractual liability unless specifically covered
- Employee injuries (covered under separate workmen compensation)

Validity: 1 year from policy start date
Premium: Rs. 25,000 per year (varies based on business size)
