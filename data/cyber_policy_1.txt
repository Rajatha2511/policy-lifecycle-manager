Cyber Insurance Policy - Digital Risk Protection
This cyber insurance policy provides comprehensive coverage against cyber threats, data breaches, ransomware attacks, and online fraud for individuals and businesses.

Key Benefits:
- Data breach response costs up to Rs. 25 lakhs
- Ransomware payment and recovery costs up to Rs. 10 lakhs
- Business interruption due to cyber attack up to Rs. 15 lakhs
- Cyber extortion and threat coverage
- Legal and regulatory fines coverage
- Public relations and reputation management costs
- 24/7 cyber incident response team support

Coverage Areas:
- Unauthorized access to computer systems
- Malware and virus attacks
- Phishing and social engineering fraud
- Online identity theft
- Website defacement and downtime
- Electronic data loss and restoration

Exclusions:
- Damage caused by own employees intentionally
- Pre-existing vulnerabilities known before policy start
- Loss due to unpatched or outdated software
- Physical damage to hardware
- Intellectual property disputes

Incident Response:
- Report cyber incident within 72 hours of discovery
- Do not pay ransom without informing insurer
- Preserve all digital evidence
- Engage approved forensic investigators only

Validity: 1 year from policy start date
Premium: Rs. 12,000 per year (individual), Rs. 50,000 per year (business)
