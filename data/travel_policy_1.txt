Travel Insurance Policy - International Travel Cover
This travel insurance policy provides comprehensive coverage for international and domestic travel including medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and lost baggage.

Key Benefits:
- Emergency medical expenses up to Rs. 50 lakhs
- Trip cancellation and curtailment coverage up to Rs. 1 lakh
- Lost baggage and personal belongings up to Rs. 50,000
- Flight delay compensation Rs. 2,000 per 6 hours delay
- Emergency evacuation and repatriation coverage
- 24/7 travel assistance helpline

Exclusions:
- Pre-existing medical conditions
- Adventure sports unless add-on purchased
- Travel to war zones or sanctioned countries
- Self-inflicted injuries
- Alcohol or drug related incidents

Validity: Per trip or annual multi-trip basis
Premium: Rs. 500 per trip (domestic), Rs. 2,000 per trip (international)
